White Space Gallery

White Space Gallery is a contemporary art space dedicated to showcasing emerging and established artists who challenge conventional perspectives. Our minimalist environment allows artworks to breathe and speak for themselves, creating an intimate dialogue between the viewer and the art. Located in the heart of Brno, we provide a sanctuary for contemplation and artistic discovery.

Evelyn Moreau

Evelyn Moreau is a French contemporary artist known for her haunting abstract compositions that explore themes of memory, solitude, and the human condition. Working primarily with dark, muted palettes punctuated by ethereal light, Moreau creates atmospheric paintings that exist between figuration and abstraction. Her works invite viewers to project their own emotions and experiences onto the canvas, making each encounter deeply personal.

Silent Silhouettes

"Silent Silhouettes" is Moreau's most ambitious exhibition to date, featuring 15 large-scale paintings that blur the boundaries between presence and absence. The series depicts shadowy figures in contemplative spaces, exploring themes of isolation in modern society and the quiet moments of introspection we all experience. Each painting serves as a mirror, reflecting not what we see, but what we feel when confronted with our own solitude.
